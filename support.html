üì∏ What makes a good photo?
  • Good lighting: Use natural light or bright indoor lighting

  • Full card visible: Ensure entire card fits in frame
  • Clear focus: Make sure image is sharp and not blurry

üéØ Understanding Your Results

üèÜ What do the PSA grades mean?
  • PSA 10 - Gem Mint: Perfect condition
  • PSA 9 - Mint: Near perfect with minor flaws
  • PSA 8 - Near Mint-Mint: Very good condition
  • PSA 7 - Near Mint: Good condition with some wear
  • PSA 6 and below: Increasing levels of wear and damage
üìê What is centering analysis?

Centering measures how well the card image is positioned within the borders:

  • Perfect centering: 50/50 on all sides
  • Good centering: 55/45 or better
  • Off-center: 60/40 or worse affects the grade
  • Left/Right vs Top/Bottom: Measured separately
